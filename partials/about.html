Questions & Answers

Do courses have a strict schedule?

Our courses are self-paced meaning that you choose when you want to learn. Stuk.io tutorials are always available online so you can view them using your desktop, laptop, tablet or smartphone as long as you have Internet connection.

Do I need to know programming basics before enrolling?

You don't need to know anything about programming before enrolling at Stuk.io. We will teach you step-by-step how to build web apps and during the process teach you all the concepts and basics.

What programming languages do you teach at Stuk.io?

We teach Ruby on Rails which is a programming framework allowing you to build things really fast and robust (e.g. Twitter, Airbnb or Github are built using Ruby on Rails). However we also cover other web technologies during our courses so you will also learn some HTML, CSS, Bootstrap concepts and techniques along the way.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my billing plan whenever I want?

Yes, you can change your plan whenever you want and changes will reflect immediately on the next recurring payment date when you downgrade and immediately when you upgrade. :)

Do I get a certificate whenever I complete a course?

Yes, you'll get a nice certificate so you can show it off on your resume or Linkedin profile.

I have an idea for a web application. Can Stuk help me build it?

Absolutely! Check out our courses/apps and see if combining some of their features together with our Codecasts you could build your dream web application. If some features are missing just request a Codecast or talk with us and we can put it on our roadmap.

On average, how long does it take me to develop each web application?

On average each course takes 1 month to complete if you dedicate 1 hour everyday to go about our content and do it yourself understanding all the bits and pieces.

What happens if I get stuck?

Don't panic! :) If you subscribed to the Starter plan you can check our Help Center and our Community forum and you'll find the answers you need. However if you're having some really tough issues you can also subscribe to Stuk.io's Developer plan and gain access to a 1-on-1 direct line with experienced developers that will help you out.
